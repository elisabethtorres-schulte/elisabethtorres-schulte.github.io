Realtime Neural Engineering Lab

This lab erased memories in rats by disrupting spikes of hippocampal activity during memory consolidation. In this role, I trained and socialized the rodent test subjects and helped make the implants. The funding grant's objective was to use these kinds of neural implants to treat PTSD in veterans.

Takeaways:This role highlight the power of invasive neuro-technologies. While I recognized the tremendous benefits the work could have, I had to confront the portntial negative uses of my work as well. This role taught me the importance of robust usage guardrails as we develop more and more powerful technologies.
