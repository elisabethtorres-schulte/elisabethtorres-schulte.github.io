Beyond Exorcism: Novel Understandings of Mental Illness Through Catholic Art

Poster & Abstract

This grant-funded project examined portrayals of saints in Catholic art who exhibited behaviors consistent with modern mental health diagnoses. By situating these portrayals in their socio-cultural context, I argued that the line between pathology and normalcy is socially contingent, heavily shaped by both societal norms and biology.
